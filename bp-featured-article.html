Why teak wood is a great choice for your home furniture

  1. Blog Posts
  2. Featured Article

Countless different materials have been used for the construction of home furniture. Wood, plastic, textiles, aluminium, and many more. While each material differs in terms of price, aesthetics and durability, wooden furniture is often regarded as the best choice for those looking to beautify your houses with a sleek, modernist twist.

Wooden furniture can be found in a number of designs, allowing you to find the perfect look to fit your personal sense of style and overall needs. Any part of the house is essentially enhanced by the warmth, comfort and effortless suave that wooden furniture provides

More than just a pretty face, no other material offers the same unique concoction of aesthetic appeal and unrivalled structural durability that wood delivers. Moreover, just about every piece of furniture in your home can be imbued with a classy and elegant wooden look. This includes tables, chairs, bedside tables, dressers and even king-size beds. Even smaller decorative pieces such as picture frames are often found in wood form

While the beauty of wooden furniture is often the first thing that comes to mind, its functional nature is just as crucial to its popularity.

Wood is robust and long-lasting, and does not easily wear out in the sun. This makes it the perfect choice for those hoping to invest in a timeless piece with longevity.

Whether you choose a hardware like teak and oak or a softwood such as cedar and pine, you can expect a well-made wooden chair or desk to come with an element of reliability and hardiness to it. This durability also makes for low-fuss maintenance. All that is needed for a quick clean is an occasional wipe down using a wet cloth. Of course, waxing, polishing and oiling will ensure further longevity for your furniture, but this is an equally straightforward process that only needs to be executed a few times a year.

Not all material has the same durable, low-maintenance effect. For example, iron furniture rusts easily if it is left in the sun. Even regular cleaning or painting of the furniture will do little to counter the corrosive process.

In terms of appearance, wooden furniture has an intrinsic timeless and traditional appeal that will never go out of style. You do not have to worry about your brand-new wooden sofa set being superseded and looking dated after a few short years, saving you the money you would otherwise have spent on making constant upgrades to keep up with the trends.

Wood is available in a gargantuan range of colours and tones, which assures variety to perfectly fit every individual look and style. This is not to mention the subtle yet perceptible differences that exist between the grains and textures of different trees and cuts.

These subtle differences mean there is a further scope for personalisation beyond the actual material. For instance, because of the grain of the wood, two pieces of wood will end up slightly different even if they are cut from the same tree and in the same direction.

A beautifully grained wood such as oak makes for a beautiful room centrepiece as a coffee table. Oak wood is available in red and white varieties; red, as the name implies, has a reddish tint, while white takes on a greyish brown appearance. When used as part of a well-designed home interior, wood can completely transform the mood of a room and even the whole house, creating a homely, welcoming atmosphere. Furthermore, because every piece of wood furniture is unique, each piece has unique qualities that make it suitable to your space and cannot be emulated anywhere else. Even in a dining set, the table and chairs will each have their own understated variances.

Before deciding on what wooden furniture to purchase, one must consider all the varieties of wood that are available and their unique characteristics. Oak and maple are widely used, while mahogany and teak the perfect choice for those with a bigger budget. Teak is incredibly durable, even relative to other varieties of wood, meaning it requires minimal maintenance if any at all. On the other hand, mahogany has a beautiful red hint within the brownness of the wood, making it ideal if you are after something more exquisite.

You can also select and combine varieties of wooden furniture, for a heterogeneous feel in your home. For instance, in a rustic-themed home, consider combining a mahogany panel bed frame with a solid pine dresser table.

This variety offers a chic finish to any home regardless of your choice.

The versatility of wood also extends to outdoor use. When it comes to your patio, wood is unmatched both in terms of its durability and attractiveness. An oak chair provides contrast yet fits in seamlessly with a gum wood deck, whilst also withstanding the harsh weather elements.

At the end of the day, wooden furniture remains a worthwhile investment in spite of any increased upfront costs. The appeal of natural wood is unmatched in terms of structural integrity and can be exceptionally easy on the eye, owing to its unique richness and glow.

Pair a wooden sofa with a glass top coffee table and the natural beauty of the material will infuse warmth and character to any room. So, you never have to worry about a wooden piece going out of style.

Coupled with the fuss-free, low-maintenance nature of wooden furniture, this ultimately makes it a worthwhile investment in the long-run.
